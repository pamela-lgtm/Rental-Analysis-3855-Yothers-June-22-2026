An Honest, Strategic Note on Layout

A true 3-bedroom that lives like a four

The public record counts four bedrooms; in practice this is a three-bedroom with a dedicated office — the fourth room functions as a flex space rather than a true bedroom. We market it precisely as what it is, because misrepresenting a layout is the fastest way to lose a qualified tenant at the showing. The advantage: that flex room is a genuine selling point. For the renter who needs a home office, a nursery, a gym, or a guest study, this property delivers a four-room footprint with the honesty of a three-bedroom listing — which lets us hold pricing near the larger comps rather than dropping to the bottom of the three-bedroom range.

Location

What makes this corner of Apopka special

This is not a typical Apopka address. Yothers Road sits in the northwest Wekiva pocket of Orange County — a place defined less by subdivisions than by spring-fed rivers, state forest, and a brand-new gateway to the rest of Orlando. While many of the comparable homes sit inside named communities nearby, your property's appeal is its setting and its access.

—— I

On-ramp to the whole region

The Wekiva Parkway (SR 429) was completed in 2024, closing the final gap in Orlando's beltway. Its Kelly Park Road interchange — with the parkway crossing Yothers Road itself — puts the property minutes from a toll-road connection to the airport, the attractions corridor, and downtown employment. This single change has transformed northwest Apopka's commute.

—— II

Springs and forest at the doorstep

Kelly Park at Rock Springs — a 68°F natural spring with a famous tubing run — is just up the road, alongside Wekiwa Springs State Park and the federally designated Wild & Scenic Wekiva River. Miles of hiking, paddling, and biking trails make this one of the most genuinely scenic residential pockets in metro Orlando.

—— III

Growth arriving now

Development is following the new road. Wekiva Ranch — 368 residences plus more than 33,000 sq ft of shops and dining on Kelly Park Road — is underway, with retail expected to begin opening in 2026. Rising amenities and rooftops lift rental demand across the immediate area.

—— IV

Freedom of no HOA

With no homeowners association and a quarter-acre lot, the property offers space, parking, and flexibility that community-restricted rentals can't — including a wider, pet-friendly tenant pool. Assigned schools are Zellwood Elementary, Wolf Lake Middle, and Apopka High.

Execution

How we lease it in under 21 days

  1. Price as the value, from day one. The data is unambiguous: homes that priced sharply at launch leased fastest at full ask. We do not test a high number and chase it down — that is how listings go stale.
  2. Market the flex room as a feature, honestly. We lead with "3 bedrooms plus a dedicated office / nursery / flex," attracting the work-from-home and growing-family tenants for whom that fourth room is the deciding factor — without any showing-day surprises.
  3. Professional photography before it goes live. The property already shows well, vacant and freshly presented. Strong photography on a sharply priced, newer home is what generates the first-week inquiry volume that produces a fast, qualified lease.
  4. Lean on the location story. Beltway access, the springs and state park, and a no-HOA lot are real differentiators against community-restricted competitors — and a pet-friendly policy widens the qualified applicant pool further.
  5. Screen for the right tenant, not the first one. A highly qualified tenant can rent anywhere in their price range; our job is to make this the property they choose — and to place one who protects your investment for the long term.
